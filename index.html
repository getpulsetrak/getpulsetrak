How it works.

Install PulseTrak

Install PulseTrak

Click the "Install PulseTrak" button on this page. Once you are redirected to the Chrome Web Store, click "Add to Chrome" to finish installing PulseTrak.

Connect your Strava Account

On the Setup Page, Click the Strava button to link your account. This will redirect you to a Strava authorization page.

Navigate to your favorite YouTube Workout Video

The PulseTrak button will load under the video next to the "Save" button

Connect Bluetooth, Click Record, Start Working out!

If you have a Bluetooth Heart Rate Monitor, Click the "Connect Bluetooth" button to pair it. Once ready, click "Record Workout", click "Play" on the video and Workout!

Complete Workout

When the video ends, your workout will auto-sync to strava. Done early? Click the "Complete Workout" button to manually sync!
